Building Instagram Bot using Selenium

Using Python Selenium library to automate the Instagram activity

This is a simple guide to create an Instagram bot to automate the processes like logging in, following, and unfollowing people on Instagram without spamming Instagram. This bot is made in python with a selenium library. The bot will save time by automating the processes and running after a random period of time. You will discover that selenium is a very powerful library and you do not need much programming experience in python to make this bot.

More About

Selenium Library

For this project, I will be using the selenium library. The other option is to use the Instagram API. However, its usage is strictly monitored to align with Instagram’s user guidelines. Selenium is a very powerful library that gives freedom from any API’s. The library was originally developed for web application testing. However, with the combination of python and selenium, one can automate the browser processes or web scraping with minimal coding knowledge.

Chrome/Firefox drivers

For the selenium library to access your browser, a web driver is necessary. I used geckodriver for my Firefox browser. Nevertheless, one can easily change to chrome by download Chromedriver. You can download geckodriver from here or Chrome Driver from here. Also, for Chrome, you will need to change webdriver. Firefox to webdriver.Chrome, and specify the path of the executable file of the web driver.

    This article will be broken up into the following steps:
  • Logging in to the account
  • Following the relevant people
  • Unfollow people over a limit

Login

The first thing we would like to do is log in to your Instagram account. I am using XPath of the elements to find the username and password field in the browser. The .click() functionality of the Selenium is used to click the element. On the login page, we use it to click the submit button. After logging in, you would be asked for turning ON the Instagram notification. The bot can easily get rid of it by clicking the “Not Now” button.

The Algorithm

I was wondering what will be the optimal algorithm to increase the followers on Instagram without spamming or getting blocked. Initially, I came up with the idea of following the people from the Instagram’s suggestion list. Nevertheless, after a few runs, Instagram started suggesting verified accounts. These are the accounts with millions of followers and the odds are very low that these accounts will follow you back. With this strategy, our bot will be used to increase the following of the verified accounts, which is futile. I had to devise another strategy to follow the people who have the maximum probability of following us back. These people are the people who share the same interest as your Instagram account.

Following

In this example, I posted supercars pictures on my account. Therefore, I made a python list of Instagram's famous supercars accounts. From the list of these accounts, the bot randomly selects a one and searches for it on Instagram. From the followers list of these accounts, the bot follows some of the people. These people have the highest odds to follow our account back. You can set the number of people to follow in one browsing session. To avoid spamming, do not follow many people at one time. After following people, the bot will close the browser and sleep for a random number of seconds given by the user input.

I use XPath to find the element. However, the XPath might change when the website is updated. It can easily found by using the inspect element → Copy XPath in the browser.

Unfollowing

Following too many people will increase your following list of the account. Therefore, to get rid of excessive following people. An unfollow function is made which can unfollow people. You can set a limit on the maximum number of following for your account. The bot will log in back to the account and check the following list of your account. If the following people are less then the user-specified limit, it will do nothing and close the browser, else it will start unfollowing people till the limit condition is satisfied and then close the browser.

Nonetheless, if you unfollow the recently followed people, then they might not get enough time to follow you back. Hence, the bot scrolls all the way down to the bottom of the following list of your account, and unfollow the old followed people by the bot.

Customize In the hashtag list, specify the accounts whose content matches the content of your account. Once the number of people to follow at one time, the maximum limit of the user’s following, and random sleep time is set. the process repeats infinitely after random sleep time between the user-specified time in seconds.
