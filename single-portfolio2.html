OrionSuit

OrionSuite is an online platform working towards automation in business & marketing technology. The currently available marketing and business technology are very expensive and complex, to make it affordable and simple we come up with the solution “OrionSuite”. Our focus is to provide an affordable marketing platform with loaded features to take any business to the pinnacle of success. In India, the number of businesses is growing at an exponential rate, additionally accepting WFH culture. To step into the market, one has to ensure the right marketing tools and the channel of communication to be robust. On the bright side, our platform is providing an online business room with a suite of tools for professional email marketing, bulk WhatsApp messaging, and also social media marketing to accelerate a business to the top. It includes the business suite for admin and sub admin controls with franchise model so one can start & track the business with CRM virtually and have their employees working from any corner of the world. For professional business a USB key token login is also available for added security.
